Checkin

A prototype application to innovate loyalty programs that inspire individuals to prioritize their health and make positive lifestyle decisions.

Overview

Today, about 1/6 people in the EU, while 1/5 people in Finland face mental health issues. Even though the EU aims to provide the best healthcare, these issues are often hard to predict and go unnoticed until the symptoms worsen. CheckIn aims to tackle this problem specifically by targeting young adults, to feel empowered and more aware about their mental state. CheckIn makes this possible by seamlessly integrating simple well-being practices into people's lives. This will allow users to feel in charge of their mental state, and the application would provide a platform for proactive, preventive, and predictive mental health awareness.

Role: Team Lead

Tools & methods: Literature Research, Research Interviews, Market Analysis

Team: Demola Project, Tampere (3 team members)

Timeline: 3 months (Oct 2023 - Dec 2023)
